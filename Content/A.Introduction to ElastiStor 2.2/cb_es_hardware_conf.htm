Simple hardware setup

The following figure illustrates a simple hardware setup, to form a HA Group, that you can use to get started:

Connect the SAS cable between the SAS Port of the Node and Disk Array's INPort . To form a daisy chain, connect SAS cable between Out Port of one Disk Array and INPort of another Disk array. This sets up a group of Nodes. An HA Group can have a maximum of four Nodes.
